About Us

Our only mission is to provide our valued clients with the most beautiful, luxurious and mind-blowing products. We specialize in designing, manufacturing, outsourcing and international shipping all around the world. We take pride in our collections and work with some of the best designers in the world. On our website, you can find pieces that range from antique-style furniture all the way to modern and contemporary statement pieces.

Style your home according to your personality, as our products have become the first choice for clients all over the globe. We specialize in offering the highest quality bespoke service with which we can make custom alterations to all of our products. Our furniture pieces speak for themselves. You will find glamorous and beautiful creations made from wood, gold leaf, silver leaf, brass, crystals, marble stone and glass. Transform your home with latest and creative pieces which are handcrafted especially for you.

We work very closely with the world’s top designers to produce the most eye-catching and unique pieces for our valued customers. Also available is a sourcing service so if you have seen a piece elsewhere that is of interest to you, simply provide us with the details and our specialist team will source it for you. The luxury furniture ranges are created by using high profile materials such as Swarovski crystals, gold leaf, Lapis Lazuli, Malachite and a wide variety of semi-precious stones which can also be used for walls and flooring. Simply browse through our Royal and Tiffany collection and see for yourself.
